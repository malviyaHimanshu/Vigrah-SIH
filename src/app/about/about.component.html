About Us

What is Vigrah?
Vigrah (विग्रह) is Sanskrit for 'decryption'
Our motto is "Preserve, Protect, Promote". Project Vigrah aims to leverage state-of-art technologies to preserve, protect and promote the epigraphical heritage of our country.

Why Vigrah?
We often find that people are clueless about our great epigraphical heritage. As per our understanding, this happens due to three reasons - One, the inaccessibility and bad condition of some inscriptions. Two, the lack of knowledge of the script. Three, the lack of literary resources for a common man to get a comprehensive understanding of the inscriptions. To address these issues, we use technological solutions.

Our tools:
Currently, we support three tools, Abhiñāna- OCR for Ashokan Brahmi script, Akṣarāntara - transliteration to a modern script and Kośa - a repository of inscriptional information.

Our long-term aims include creating an end-to-end technology solution for Indian epigraphy.
